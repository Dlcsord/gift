Scams can look pretty convinving sometimes. Here are some things to look out for:
  • Notice the button placement. (or is there a button at all?)
  • Discord Gifts are not just images! Only a portion of a gift is an image while the rest is made up of fancy website code.
  • Spelling mistakes. (not sure how often these occur.)
